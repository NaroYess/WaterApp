Social Sharing. This plugin allows a person to share text, images and links. This could be useful to share water and health progress with friends on social media. This plugin allows you to use the native sharing window of your mobile device. The sharing options for the plugin depend on what has been setup in the device settings. Local Notification. This plugin allows local notifications. Could be useful to remind users to drink water. Notifications can be used as an alert or banner, badging the app’s icon or playing a sound. The main purpose of this plugin is to inform its users that it has something waiting for them. Screenshot. The screenshot plugin allows your app to take screenshots. The pics are stored in the devices storage.
